ABOUT PROJECT::PERSONALISATION

Want a guided and personalised experience? Tell us your goals and we will tailor your learning journey to fit your needs.

It is sometimes difficult to decide where you want to start when you only have a vague idea of what you want to do in the end. With our personalisation feature, just tell us what you want to learn at the end of your journey or what project you want to build towards and we will craft a project track for you tailor-made to your requirements. Depending on your needs, we will either help you select a project track that suits your needs from our Tech-Tree or craft a project track from scratch for you and send you project::boxes once every few days or weeks, depending on what you prefer. Every personalised track comes with a generous amount of mentorship time so you get all the guidance you need.

Why it works:

  • We help you ensure that the track you take is guaranteed to meet your learning objectives, from building a cool SumoBot to challenge your friends with to picking up the required skills and concepts for your schooling needs.
  • Get one-to-one help and even instruction booklets tailored to you with personalisation so that you can be sure that you understand concepts and pick up skills as quickly as possible

Have an idea for a project::box that you can't find on our tech tree but do not want to subscribe to a personalised track?

Submit your project idea to us and receive a Box based on your accepted suggestion containing the complete set of required parts, completely free regardless of total price. We will even integrate your suggestion into the tech tree and ensure that your suggestion is part of a learning pathway so that you can make even more cool projects!
