Keep track of your inventory. Save money by only getting the parts you need.

KITS::INVENTORY PORTAL

Automatically curated for a hassle-free experience

The Inventory portal shows the components that you have based on your past purchases. If you want to use self-purchased parts in one of our projects or if one of your parts has met its end, keep your list updated by adding or removing it from the portal.

Why it works:

  • Automatically updated from every purchase so you don't have to.
  • Pictures of components; so you can be sure that the inventory is correct.
  • Simple functionality (add or remove parts); so you won't get lost in an ocean of features.
ORDER REVIEW PORTAL

Tell us what you have and leave the rest to us.

For every order, you don't have to care about what parts you need to buy to make the project. Simply check that the inventory is up to date and we will automatically tell you what parts you need when you place an order.

Why it works:

  • Automatic parts list at order, so there is no confusion.
  • Detailed breakdown of all the costs, so that you know where your money is going.
  • Changeable parts list, so that you can buy extra parts if you need them.

With Inventory, you can declutter your cabinet and save your dollars by only paying for what you need - learning need not be expensive, messy and stressful.
