We Have a Project For Everybody

Regardless of whether you have prior experiences, are just looking to do a fun project or want a guided introductory experience into Arduinos, we got you covered. Make use of our Series and Tracks to choose the most appropriate project for you to get started with.

Project Series

Projects in the same series represent a group of projects that teach similar concepts of the same difficulty level. For example, the Whimsical Series teaches the fundamental Arduino concepts that you will use in all your future projects.

Project Tracks

Projects that are lined in a track will allow you to progress from beginner to competent as you pick up skills and learn concepts along the way and build cool projects. Finish off every track with a challenging capstone project like an Epic Gamer Mouse is sure to put your knowledge and skills to the test.

Meet the Whimsical Series

Learn the foundational software and hardware concepts of Arduino programming. Go for the Final Cut version of the instruction booklet for a more formal and technical explanation or Ardy's Cut to explore these concepts through the whimsical world of Ardy and his friends in a more light-hearted manner.

Useless Light Meter

Get comfortable with Arduino programming by writing your first piece of "Hello World" code and learning about analog and digital inputs and outputs, variables, operators, data types and conditional statements.

Myopic Sun Follower

Get your first taste of writing a full-fledged algorithm: conditionally control your robot by playing around with servos and understanding the logic behind certain built-in functions of Arduino.
